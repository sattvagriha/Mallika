About Mallika Soap

Mallika Herbal Soap is enriched with Raktachandana and Yashtimadhu Kwatha, blended with Vitamin E, Olive Oil, and Glycerin. Crafted with minimal preservatives, it is safe for all skin types and delivers authentic Ayurvedic care for everyday use.

Method of Preparation

  • Melt soap base using double boiler method.
  • Mix Raktachandana & Yashtimadhu Kwatha with Vitamin E capsule.
  • Blend mixture into the soap base and pour into molds.
  • (Optional) Add 2 drops of natural fragrance oil.
  • Spray isopropyl alcohol before & after to avoid bubbles.
